Here's what changed from v2 → v3:

**Bracketing runs in its own thread now.** The stream never freezes during capture. `bracket_loop()` runs independently as a daemon thread, with a `bracket_lock` to prevent overlapping sets if the interval is shorter than capture time.

**Removed the 3-second shot delay.** The settle frames (now 4 by default) plus a brief 0.3s `BRACKET_POST_SETTLE` pause is all you need. A full 3-shot bracket now takes ~2–4s instead of ~12s.

**No more manual gain boost.** Gain is locked at whatever auto-exposure had settled to before the bracket starts. This avoids the noise bomb from `AnalogueGain: 10.0` + long shutter in v2.

**Added a `relative` bracket mode.** You can switch `BRACKET_MODE = "relative"` to auto-scale from current exposure (useful if lighting conditions change), or keep `"fixed"` for repeatable lab conditions.

**Metadata JSON sidecar.** Each bracket set saves a `.json` file with requested vs. actual exposure, gain, timestamps, and resolution — useful when you're building your training dataset and need to track what the sensor actually did.
